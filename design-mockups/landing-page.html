FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to get my headshots?
Most headshots are ready within 5 minutes. Our AI processes your photos quickly while maintaining high quality. You'll receive an email notification when they're ready.
What kind of photo should I upload?
Upload a clear, well-lit photo where your face is clearly visible. A simple selfie works great! Avoid heavy filters, sunglasses, or photos where your face is partially hidden.
Can I use these for LinkedIn and professional profiles?
Absolutely! Our headshots are specifically designed for professional use. They're perfect for LinkedIn, company websites, resumes, email signatures, and any professional profile.
What if I'm not satisfied with the results?
We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If you're not happy with your headshots for any reason, contact us within 7 days for a full refund, no questions asked.
Is my photo data secure?
Yes, your privacy is our priority. We use secure encryption for all uploads and processing. Your photos are automatically deleted from our servers after 30 days, and we never share your data with third parties.
